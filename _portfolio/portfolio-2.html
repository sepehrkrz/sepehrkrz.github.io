The project involves the development of a Tableau dashboard to visualize the results of an extensive Twitter data analysis focused on the 2018 California Campfire. The analysis encompasses sentiment analysis, topic modeling, and emotion analysis, capturing the public's reactions and discussions across the entire United States. This interactive dashboard allows users to explore state-level insights, revealing how different regions responded emotionally and thematically to the disaster. By providing a comprehensive view of the sentiment and key topics prevalent during the event, the dashboard aims to facilitate a deeper understanding of public opinion and social dynamics in the context of natural disasters.
